## Overview

Sequa builds a real-time knowledge graph from your code repositories and tickets. You gain instant answers to accelerate development. Follow these steps to set up your account and connect sources in minutes.

<Callout kind="tip">
  You complete setup in under 10 minutes. No credit card required for the free tier.
</Callout>

## Step-by-Step Setup

Use these steps to create your account, configure the dashboard, connect data sources, and run your first query.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://app.sequa.ai` and click **Sign Up**.

    Fill in your email, team name, and password. Verify your email via the confirmation link.

    After login, you land on the dashboard with a guided tour.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Initial Dashboard" icon="settings">
    Customize your workspace:

    - Set your team timezone in **Settings > General**.
    - Enable notifications for query responses in **Settings > Notifications**.
    - Review default knowledge graph settings under **Graph > Sources**.

    Save changes to apply them instantly.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Repositories" icon="git-branch">
    Link your GitHub or GitLab repositories to index code automatically.

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="GitHub" icon="github">
        Go to **Integrations > GitHub** and click **Connect**.

        Authorize Sequa with your GitHub account. Select repositories like `your-org/webapp` and `your-org/api`.

        Sequa indexes commits, PRs, and code files within 2 minutes.
      </Tab>

      <Tab title="GitLab" icon="git-commit">
        Navigate to **Integrations > GitLab** and **Connect**.

        Paste your GitLab personal access token (create one at `https://gitlab.com/-/profile/personal_access_tokens` with `read_repository` scope).

        Choose projects such as `your-group/frontend` and confirm.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Tickets" icon="ticket">
    Integrate issue trackers to include tickets in your knowledge graph.

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Jira" icon="layers">
        In **Integrations > Jira**, enter your site URL like `https://yourcompany.atlassian.net`.

        Provide API token and email. Select projects like `PROJ-KEY`.

        Sync starts immediately for issues, comments, and epics.
      </Tab>

      <Tab title="Linear" icon="list">
        Head to **Integrations > Linear** and **Connect with OAuth**.

        Grant access to teams such as `engineering`. Sequa pulls cycles, issues, and updates.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Run Your First Query" icon="search">
    Test the knowledge graph with natural language queries.

    Open the search bar and try these examples:

    <CodeGroup tabs="Code Query,Ticket Query">
      ```plaintext
      How do I implement JWT authentication in our Node.js API at /auth/routes?
      ```

      ```plaintext
      What blockers are on the login feature in Jira PROJ-123?
      ```
    </CodeGroup>

    Sequa returns linked code snippets, tickets, and explanations. Refine with follow-ups like "Show the middleware code".
  </Step>
</Steps>
